Title: Britain one of least ‘nature-connected’ nations in world – with Nepal the most

Summary: <p>Others languishing near bottom of 61-country study include Canada, Germany, Israel, Japan and Spain</p><p>Britain is one of the least “nature connected” nations in the world, according to the first ever global study of how people relate to the natural world.</p><p>Britain ranks 55th out of 61 countries in the study of 57,000 people, which looks at how attitudes towards nature are shaped by social, economic, geographical and cultural factors.</p> <a href="https://www.theguardian.com/environment/2025/nov/01/britain-one-of-least-nature-connected-nations-in-world-with-nepal-the-most">Continue reading...</a>
