Title: Rithm Capital CEO on NYC Mayoral Race, CRE Investments

Summary: Michael Nierenberg, Rithm Capital CEO, says there are “a lot of concerns” around Mamdani. He tells Katie Greifeld and Romaine Bostick on “The Close” that while Rithm doesn’t own any rent-stabilized housing, that sector would likely come under pressure if Mamdani wins the election. (Source: Bloomberg)
