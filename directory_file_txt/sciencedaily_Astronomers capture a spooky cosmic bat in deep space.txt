Title: Astronomers capture a spooky “cosmic bat” in deep space

Summary: Astronomers have captured a haunting image of a “cosmic bat” spreading its wings across deep space. This nebula, 10,000 light-years away, glows crimson as newborn stars ignite clouds of gas and dust.
