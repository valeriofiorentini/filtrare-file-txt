Title: Mass Rally Marks a Year of Grief and Protests That Shook Serbia

Summary: Tens of thousands marched through the Serbian city of Novi Sad to mark the anniversary of a fatal train station incident that has spawned a wave of anti-government protests, fueled political tensions and deepened divisions in the year since it rocked the Balkan country.
