Title: Macau Gaming Revenue Beats Estimate on Post-Golden Week Boost

Summary: Macau’s gaming revenue rose 15.9% in October, exceeding analyst expectations as the world’s biggest gambling hub experienced a rebound in players after China’s Golden Week holiday was foiled by bad weather.
